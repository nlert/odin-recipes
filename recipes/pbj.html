Peanut Butter and Jelly

This is what the finished sandwich should look like.

This recipe is very complex but if you are up for the challenge then you will be nicely rewarded in the end.

Ingredients

  • Bread (2 Slices)
  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly

Steps

  1. Take two slices of your favorite bread and put them on a plate
  2. Evenly spread peanut butter on one slice
  3. Spread your favorite jelly on the other slice
  4. Place one piece of bread on top of the other (Warning: Make sure the clean sides of the bread are on the outside)
