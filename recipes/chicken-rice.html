Chciken and Rice

This is what the finished plate should look like.

This dish is very simple. However, it is very healthy while still tasting great.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • Brown Rice
  • Salt, pepper and whatever seasoning you want

Steps

  1. Take your chicken breasts and season them while they are raw
  2. Put them in the air fryer on 385 degrees (F) for 8-10 minutes
  3. Put the rice in the rice cooker
